Betta tank mates

While betta fish can be aggresive little munchkins, that doesn't mean they can't have friends! Below is a list of some of the most popular betta safe tank mates. Enjoy!

  • African dwarf frogs
  • Neon tetras
  • Kuhli loaches
  • ember tetras
  • Mystery snails
  • Otocinclus catfish
  • Harlequin rasboras
  • Cory catfish
  • Female guppies
  • Platys
  • Rummy-nosed tetras
