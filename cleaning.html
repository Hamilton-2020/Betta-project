Tank cleaning guide

You will need: An algae scrubber, a tank siphon, lime cleaner, bleach, clean filter cartridge, and a aquarium brush

At first, this guide may seem complex or even scary, but once you get the hang of it, it's easy!

  • First, prepare the tank for cleaning. Gather your supplies and have them nearby.
  • Next, get your betta and safely transport him/her to a bowl or temporary tank
  • Scrub the inside of the tank with the scrubber and get all off the algae spots
  • Soak the aquarium decorations in bleach for an hour. Make sure to get ALL of the bleach off before putting the decorations back in
  • Siphon away all of the dirty water from the gravel and dump it out
  • Use aquarium safe lime cleaner on the outside of the tank to clean the glass
  • Change the filter cartridge
  • Put your betta back in its home
  • You're done!
