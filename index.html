    • Why Bettas?

    Bettas are wonderful, gorgeous, and intelligent creatures. Aside from their distinctive silky fins, they are complex and emotional creatures. They can even be taught tricks! This is why it's very sad every time a betta is forced to live in a small, cramped, fishbowl that does not allow them to swim happily and be the unique, special, individuals that they are. That's why I made this website, To help people with any kind of betta question!
